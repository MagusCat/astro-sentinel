# Sentinel Overview

**Sentinel** is a modern, web-based academy management platform built to streamline the daily operations of dance academies, pole sport studios, and recreational sports centers. 

## Target Audience
The system is designed for non-technical users, primarily academy owners, receptionists, and instructors, who need a low learning-curve solution to manage their business efficiently. The interface is optimized for both desktop PCs and tablets, prioritizing large, touch-friendly interactions.

## Key Features
- **User & Membership Management**: Complete profile management with lifecycle tracking (Active, Expired, Suspended, Frozen).
- **Operational Management**: Instructor assignments, schedule tracking, and comprehensive payment systems.
- **Class Management**: Capacity limits, overbooking prevention, and real-time availability.
- **Retention & Communication**: Automated alerts for debt, membership expiration, and mass notifications.
- **Reports & Analytics**: High-level executive dashboards displaying revenue, growth, and active member metrics.

## UX Philosophy
The user experience is built on the following principles:
- **Simplicity & Speed**: Clean navigation, step-by-step flows, and zero clutter.
- **Accessibility**: High contrast, readable typography, and large touch targets.
- **Premium Feel**: Soft shadows, minimal borders, dynamic micro-interactions, and a sophisticated color palette without feeling overly corporate.
